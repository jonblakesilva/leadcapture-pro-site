// how_it_works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

No copy-paste. No switching tabs. No manual data entry. LeadCapture Pro eliminates the entire workflow gap between finding a lead and capturing it.

01

Browse normally

Visit any website — Google Maps, Yelp, BBB, LinkedIn, Yellow Pages, Groupon, or any local directory. The extension runs silently in the background.

02

Contacts detected instantly

Click the LeadCapture Pro button. The sidebar opens and shows all detected phones, emails, business names, and addresses — ready to review in seconds.

03

Push to GoHighLevel

Review the contact, pick a pipeline and workflow, then hit Push. The contact appears in your GHL account instantly — with tags, source notes, and pipeline stage already set.

Built for agencies
running at scale.

Every feature was designed for real GHL users who capture hundreds of leads a week — not just one at a time.

  • ✓ Batch push — select 20 contacts and push all at once with shared tags and notes
  • ✓ Auto-detects if a contact already exists in GHL before creating a duplicate
  • ✓ Workflow enrollment — enroll contacts in any GHL automation the moment they're pushed
  • ✓ Custom agency branding — your name, logo, and colors in the sidebar
  • ✓ Phone numbers normalized to E.164 automatically — no more GHL 400 errors

Auto Notes

// ghl_native

GoHighLevel-
native from day one.

LeadCapture Pro speaks GHL's API fluently. It's not a Zapier bridge or a webhook hack — it pushes directly to GHL's v2 API with the right field mappings, every time.

  • ✓ Creates contacts with proper name split (first/last)
  • ✓ Adds pipeline opportunities in your chosen stage
  • ✓ Enrolls in GHL workflows with event start time
  • ✓ Applies tags from your GHL tag library
  • ✓ Attaches a source note with URL, platform, and date
  • ✓ Detects duplicate contacts before creating
  • ✓ Rate-limited to 10 req/s — GHL-safe
