LeadCapture Pro — Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 7, 2026

Plain-English summary: LeadCapture Pro sends contact data only to your own GoHighLevel account. We have no servers that receive your data. Your API key never leaves your browser except to contact GoHighLevel directly. We collect nothing about your browsing habits.

1. Who We Are

LeadCapture Pro ("the Extension," "we," "our") is a Chrome Extension for capturing business contact information directly into GoHighLevel CRM. For support inquiries, contact us at support@leadcapturepro.io.

2. What the Extension Does

LeadCapture Pro is a browser extension that detects business contact information — phone numbers, email addresses, business names, and physical addresses — visible on webpages you visit, and allows you to push that information directly into your own GoHighLevel CRM account.

The Extension operates entirely client-side in your browser. It does not have its own servers, databases, or backend infrastructure.

3. Data We Detect (But Do Not Store)

When you visit a webpage, the Extension scans the visible page content for business contact information. This detected data:

  • Is held temporarily in your browser's local memory while the sidebar is open
  • Is written to chrome.storage.local in your browser, keyed by tab ID, for the duration of your session on that tab
  • Is deleted from local storage when you navigate away, close the tab, or close the sidebar
  • Is never transmitted to any server operated by us
  • Is transmitted to GoHighLevel's API only when you explicitly click "Push to GHL"

We do not have a server that receives or stores any contact data you capture. Every contact you push goes directly from your browser to your GoHighLevel account.

4. Data Stored in Your Browser

The Extension stores the following settings locally in Chrome's sync storage (chrome.storage.sync). This data syncs across your signed-in Chrome browsers via Google's infrastructure:

Data Item Purpose Who Can Access It
GHL API Key Authenticate requests to the GoHighLevel API on your behalf Only the Extension's background service worker. Never content scripts, never our servers.
GHL Location ID Identify which GoHighLevel sub-account to push contacts to Same as above
Agency branding (name, logo URL, color) Display your agency branding in the sidebar Only the Extension
Default pipeline, stage, and tags Pre-fill your preferred CRM workflow settings Only the Extension

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party other than GoHighLevel (your API key) and Google's Chrome Sync infrastructure (all settings, as part of Chrome's built-in sync feature).

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party
  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking of any kind
  • We do not store contact data on our servers (we have none for this purpose)
  • We do not use cookies
  • We do not display advertising
  • We do not share your GHL API key with anyone
  • We do not access webpages you visit unless you are actively using the Extension sidebar

6. Third-Party Services

GoHighLevel (HighLevel Inc.)
When you click "Push to GHL," contact data is transmitted directly from your browser to GoHighLevel's API at services.leadconnectorhq.com using HTTPS. This data goes into your own GoHighLevel account. GoHighLevel's handling of that data is governed by GoHighLevel's Privacy Policy. We are not affiliated with GoHighLevel and do not receive any of this data.

Google Chrome Sync
Extension settings stored in chrome.storage.sync are synced across your Chrome browsers by Google's infrastructure if you are signed into Chrome. This sync is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can disable Chrome Sync at any time in Chrome settings.

7. Data Security

  • Your GHL API key is stored in chrome.storage.sync, which Chrome encrypts at rest
  • All communication with GoHighLevel's API uses HTTPS (TLS encrypted)
  • The Extension's architecture separates credential access from page content: only the background service worker reads your API key, and content scripts never have access to it
  • Detected contact data is stored in tab-scoped local storage and cleared automatically when the tab session ends

8. Children's Privacy

LeadCapture Pro is a professional business tool intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used this Extension and provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • You can view and edit all stored settings at any time via the Extension's Settings page
  • You can delete all locally stored data by going to Settings → Advanced, or by uninstalling the Extension
  • Uninstalling the Extension removes all locally stored data immediately
  • Contact data you have already pushed to GoHighLevel is subject to your own GoHighLevel account settings and GoHighLevel's data policies

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we will display a notice within the Extension. Your continued use of the Extension after any change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
